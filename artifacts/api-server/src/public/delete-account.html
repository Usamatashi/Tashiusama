Delete your Tashi account

App: Tashi · Developer: Tashi

You can request permanent deletion of your Tashi account and the data associated with it at any time. There are two ways to do this:

Option 1 — Delete from inside the app

  1. Open the Tashi app on your device.
  2. Sign in to the account you want to delete.
  3. Go to Settings → Account.
  4. Tap Delete account and confirm.

Option 2 — Email us to request deletion

If you can't sign in, send an email to support@tashi.app from the email address linked to your account, with the subject Account deletion request. Include your account phone number or username so we can locate it.

We will process your request within 30 days.

What gets deleted

When your account is deleted, we permanently remove:

  • Your profile (name, phone number, email, profile photo)
  • Your scan history, claims, points and rewards balance
  • Your QR codes, orders and saved addresses
  • Push-notification tokens linked to your devices
  • Photos and files you uploaded through the app

What we may keep, and for how long

We keep a limited amount of data after deletion only when we are required to by law or to protect our service:

  • Transaction and payment records — kept for up to 7 years to comply with tax and accounting laws.
  • Fraud-prevention and abuse signals (e.g. records of banned devices) — kept for up to 2 years.
  • Anonymised, aggregated analytics that can no longer identify you — kept indefinitely.

All other personal data is removed within 30 days of your request.

Questions? Contact us at support@tashi.app.
